PROJECT STORY

SynaptiQ started as three separate data science projects — an image safety classifier, a car recommendation engine, and a supply chain demand forecaster. The idea was simple: bring them together under one unified, polished platform that demonstrates how AI can be applied to solve real-world problems. Built using TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, and FastAPI, every model is trained on real data and served live through a REST API.
